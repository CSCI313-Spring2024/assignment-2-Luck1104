Introduction

Welcome! I'm Aiden Rinas, a computer science student at NDSU. I was born and raised in Fargo, North Dakota. All through my childhood, I've had a constant interest in both video games and the internet, much like other members of my generation. Despite an initial interest in sports, I eventually started to dive into the world of computers around high school. Since then, I have taken many important beginning steps on my journey to getting my bachelor's degree in computer science. My future career goal is to land a job developing, programming, and maintaining software, though I will attempt to get an internship first as a way to get my foot in the door.
